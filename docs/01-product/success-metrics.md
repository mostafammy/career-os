# CareerOS Success Metrics

**Status:** Canonical
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Philosophy of Success

Our approach to measuring success is derived directly from our Product Principles (specifically **P-001** and **P-011**). 

> **Success is measured by improvements in users' careers—not by time spent inside the application.**

We explicitly **reject** traditional engagement vanity metrics such as:
- Time spent in the app (We want to *reduce* time spent organizing, optimizing for flow).
- Number of clicks or screens visited.
- Meaningless daily active use that doesn't tie to career progress.

Instead, CareerOS succeeds when users become better at building careers.

---

## North Star Metric

**Career Capital Growth**
The overarching indicator of success is the accumulation of reusable career assets and compounding knowledge over time.
* **Measurement:** The rate at which users generate reusable assets (e.g., reflections, document templates, knowledge entries) and successfully leverage them in new opportunities.

---

## Core Product Metrics

### 1. Opportunity Execution (Miss Fewer Opportunities)
- **Deadline Adherence Rate:** Percentage of tracked opportunities that are submitted on or before their deadlines.
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of captured opportunities that move from "Captured" to "Actioned/Applied".

### 2. Efficiency & Flow (Spend Less Time Organizing)
- **Time-to-Capture:** Average time taken to capture a new opportunity from an external source into the system.
- **Context Switching Reduction:** Decrease in the number of times a user needs to leave the Opportunity Workspace to find relevant context or documents.

### 3. Knowledge Compounding (Reuse More Previous Work)
- **Asset Reuse Rate:** The frequency with which existing knowledge entries, templates, or documents are attached to or utilized in new opportunities.
- **Reflection Completion Rate:** The percentage of completed or closed opportunities that have a corresponding post-action reflection or decision journal entry.

### 4. User Confidence & Strategy (Make Better Decisions)
- **High-Impact Prioritization:** Percentage of opportunities marked as "high strategic fit" that reach the execution phase vs. low-fit opportunities.
- **Decision Journal Utilization:** The volume of decisions actively logged and reviewed over time, indicating a shift from reactive applying to intentional strategy.

---

## System Health & Trust Metrics

To support the product principles of Trust (P-010) and Performance, we track:
- **Zero Data Loss:** 100% preservation of user-entered data and relationships.
- **Uptime & Reliability:** 99.9% availability, ensuring the user's OS is always accessible when they need to make a decision.
- **Sync/Import Success Rate:** High reliability in capturing data from external sources (e.g., browser extensions, email integrations).

---

## Review Cycle

These metrics should be reviewed quarterly to ensure they still align with the CareerOS vision of building career capital and improving decision-making quality. If a metric begins to drive behavior that contradicts our Product Principles, it must be deprecated or revised.
